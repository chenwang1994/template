Introduction

First of all, I would like to share my experience with marketing major and my thought. As many people said that Many students come to higher education with only a vague idea of what it means to become a participating member of an academic discipline. The major I have chosen is marketing currently. Actually, I don’t know what marketing is before I took marketing class in my high school. My father, who is a businessman, started his business when I was two years old. His major business is selling art craft. I think the part of reason that I chose marketing major was influenced by my father. When I was about fifteen years old, I asked my father why he would engage in this business field, because he never went to any business school, and he got his first job which was sale man in local mall when he was sixteen years old. Without any education background and working experiences, he felt everything was new to him, also felt tired. He had worst sales performance and he was trying to find reason and analysis how to let customer pay his product rather than others who have same quality but different brand products. He thought his sales strategy was singleness and his eloquence need to be improved, so he started doing the eloquence practice during his spare time and spent more time on analysis the features of each product in order to persuade customer pay his products. I was amazed by the story of my farther, and he told me a word, which I could never forget : I can not change the direction of the wind, but I can adjust my sails to always reach my destination.” I could always remember this every time when I in the slump. I think that I grow up with my father’s company, I was familiar with the products in his company.
